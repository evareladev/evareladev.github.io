    Technology-driven management models for the development of smart societies (In Spanish)

    Published in Institutional Repository of the University of Alicante, 2023

    Abstract. In recent years, humanity has witnessed significant changes occurring in society, where innovation based on scientific knowledge and the rapid pace of technological development have become fundamental pillars of contemporary society. These elements have successfully integrated and exerted a strong influence at virtually all levels and sectors of society. While the widespread adoption of technology has enabled opportunities for substantial improvements in quality of life, individual well-being, access to basic services, and overall societal development, many complex situations still exist that pose real challenges. These challenges have not been adequately addressed through traditional work models and currently available tools.

    Therefore, it is necessary to define a consistent course of action for the future, with the aim of leveraging existing opportunities and gradually overcoming the complex issues that hinder the development process of society. The foundation of this work is to delve deeper into this path in order to better understand the complex and multidimensional changes taking place in society due to the influence of technology on human interactions, and how technologies can be used to develop innovative work models that contribute to achieving sustainability goals and improvements in quality of life.

    Accordingly, this work proposes and develops the concept of the smart society, a social model centered on the needs and interests of individuals. It advocates for sustainable progress by leveraging the opportunities enabled by new technologies to establish innovative strategies based on collaborative work and the distribution of responsibilities among all involved social actors, with special emphasis on strengthening citizen participation. Citizens are positioned as the primary force for addressing the problems and challenges that society must overcome..

    Recommended citation: Varela, E. G. (2023). Modelos de gestión impulsados por la tecnología para el desarrollo de sociedades inteligentes. http://rua.ua.es/dspace/handle/10045/138287
